Hany Ragab

I am a PhD student at VUSec a.k.a. the Systems and Network Security Group at Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam.
Previously, I achieved my MSc while working in the Laboratory of Information and Network Security (LASER) at University of Milan. I also worked as a security research scholar at the Systems Software & Security Laboratory at GaTech and as a security research intern in the LCA Lab at EPFL. In my research I like to work on system security, hardware security, fuzzing, microarchitectural attacks and operating systems.

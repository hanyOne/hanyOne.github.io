Hany Ragab

I am a MSc student in the Computer Security Laboratory at University of Milan.
I'm currently working as a security research scholar in the Systems Software & Security Laboratory at Georgia Institute of Technology.
In summer 2017 I worked as a security research intern in the Laboratory for Communications and Applications at École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne. In spring 2017 I was the TA of the undergraduate Laboratory of Operating Systems. In 2015 I was a cryptography research intern in the Advanced-Systems-Technologies Security team at ST Microelectronics. Until December 2014 I worked in the Cryptography and Coding Laboratory at University of Milan where I obtained my BSc.
I partecipate FTTT in some CTFs with my lab's CTF team.

In my research I like to work on system security, hardware security, microarchitectural attacks, side-channels, trusted execution environments and operating system.
